About Mijn Wensch!

Mijn Wensch is a modern development project located at Groot St. Joris which will consist of 50 lots. The average size of the lots is between 407 and 873 m2. At this development project we give you the opportunity to buy a lot and build your own dream house on it. The lots allow enough space for a carport or even a beautiful swimming pool. Or you can start small and expand as your family grows.
